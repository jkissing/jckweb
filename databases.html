Component Databases

ToxoDB

The Toxoplasma Genomics Resource for Toxoplasma gondii and coccidian parasites.

CryptoDB

Genomic resources for Cryptosporidium species and comparative datasets.

PlasmoDB

The Plasmodium Genomics Resource for malaria parasites and related organisms.

TriTrypDB

Trypanosomatid parasite genomes including Trypanosoma and Leishmania.

AmoebaDB

Resources for Entamoeba and other amoebozoan parasites.

GiardiaDB

Comprehensive genomic data for Giardia lamblia.

MicrosporidiaDB

Genomic resources for microsporidian parasites.

PiroplasmaDB

Data for piroplasm parasites including Babesia and Theileria.

TrichDB

Genomic resources for Trichomonas vaginalis.
VB

VectorBase

Genomic data for invertebrate vectors of human pathogens.
H

HostDB

Host genome information to support host-pathogen interaction studies.
